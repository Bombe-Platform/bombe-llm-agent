# User-Queryable Datasets

This document lists the basic datasets that users can request through the Bombe LLM chatbot service.

---

## What You Can Ask About

### 1. Persona Distributions by Location

Query persona percentages and concentrations across different geographic areas:

- **National** - UK-wide persona statistics
- **Regional** - Persona breakdowns by major UK regions (e.g., London, North West, South East)
- **Local Authority** - Persona data by council/borough (e.g., Manchester, Birmingham, Camden)
- **Ward** - Persona distribution at electoral ward level
- **Constituency** - Persona data by parliamentary constituency
- **Postcode** - Persona breakdowns for specific postcodes (e.g., E11 3QA, M1 1AA)

### 2. Demographic Personas (9 categories)

Ask about people grouped by demographic characteristics:

- Persona 1 through Persona 9
- Each persona represents a distinct demographic segment
- View which personas are most prevalent in specific areas
- Compare persona distributions across locations

### 3. Commercial Personas (7 categories)

Ask about people grouped by shopping habits:

- Bombe 1 through Bombe 7
- Each Bombe category represents shopping behavior patterns
- View commercial persona concentrations by geography
- Compare shopping behaviors across regions

### 4. Behavioral Models & Predictions

Query what drives specific behaviors or activities:

- **By Persona** - How likely each persona is to engage in specific behaviors (e.g., "Which personas visit Borough Market?")
- **By Factors** - What factors influence behaviors for specific personas (e.g., "What makes Persona 3 shop at Aldi?")
- **General Behaviors** - Factors affecting activities regardless of persona (e.g., "What drives Ryanair usage?")

### 5. Geographic Comparisons

Compare data between different locations:

- Compare persona distributions between cities
- Compare regional differences
- Identify areas with highest/lowest concentrations of specific personas

### 6. Persona Characteristics

Learn about what defines each persona:

- Persona descriptions
- Persona labels and names
- Demographic vs Commercial categorization

### 7. Location-Specific Insights

Get detailed breakdowns for specific areas:

- Top personas in a city or region
- Persona diversity in an area
- Geographic patterns and trends

## Example Questions You Can Ask

1. "What are the top 5 personas in London?"
2. "Which areas have the highest concentration of Bombe 2?"
3. "Compare commercial personas between Manchester and Birmingham"
4. "What behavioral models are associated with Camden Market?"
5. "Show me Persona 3 distribution across the North West"
6. "Which postcodes in London are most likely to visit Borough Market?"
7. "What are the shopping habits of people in postcode E11 3QA?"
8. "Which local authorities have the highest percentage of affluent personas?"
9. "What drives shopping at Tesco across different personas?"
10. "Tell me about the demographic makeup of the South East region"

---

## What Makes Someone Part of a Persona?

- **Demographic Personas (1-9):** Based on demographic and commercial variables
- **Commercial Personas (Bombe 1-7):** Based on shopping habits
- **Everyone belongs to exactly ONE demographic persona**
- **Everyone belongs to exactly ONE commercial persona**

---

## Types of Analysis Available

1. **Distribution Analysis** - Where personas are concentrated
2. **Comparative Analysis** - How areas differ in persona makeup
3. **Behavioral Analysis** - What drives specific activities or purchases
4. **Predictive Insights** - Likelihood of behaviors by persona or location
5. **Geographic Profiling** - Detailed persona breakdowns for specific locations
